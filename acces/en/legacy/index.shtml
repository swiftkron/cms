About

Please join us in Scottsdale, AZ as the Act! and Swiftpage nations converge for the 2014 Act! Global Conference Nov 6th – 9th, 2014. Act! welcomes our entire ACC partner community to join thought leaders and key developer personnel, convened to collaborate, cross-train, entertain, and be inspired.

Conference

Join us for our main 3-day event which will include in-depth product, commercial and international sessions, as well as enlightening topics with plenty of opportunities for networking and connecting.

  • Share our strategy, our new product roadmaps, our new levels of innovation and our energy to work together on a fast track to growth
  • Discuss how we can align our businesses to ensure our future growth together
  • Meet key members of our Swiftpage business including the Executive Team: Chairman Bob Ogdon, CEO John Oechsle, and SVP & General Manager Bob Anderson
  • Network and develop relationships with ACC’s and developers from around the world
  • Meet with ‘Swifties’ from our executive, sales, technical, and marketing teams
  • Meet with Act! product specialists and discuss any issues you have
  • Check out the exhibitor area, network with other business partners and visit our add-on marketplace and development partners

Tracy Bullock

President, Bullock Training & Development, Inc.

Tracy Bullock is the President of Bullock Training & Development, Inc., an authorized Sandler Training® center. She delivered strategic business development with Procter & Gamble for almost 30 years in the U.S. and globally, and now works with businesses owners and managers to accelerate their competitive advantage in the market.

Her tremendous sales experience has been uniquely supplemented by her variety of other P&G roles, including Operations, Information Technology, and Capability Development. This broad range of assignments allows for innovation in all aspects of joint business with customers.

Tracy was presented a Lifetime Achievement Award, Top Female Executive, and noted as a Top 101 Industry Experts for her work in Global management Training Development by Cambridge’s Worldwide Who’s Who.

Hotel & Travel Information

The Hotel

The McCormick Scottsdale is an idyllic retreat that features stunning lakeside views in a resort setting. Situated just minutes from Phoenix, Arizona and three short miles from Old Town Scottsdale, The McCormick Scottsdale captures the spirit of warm southwestern hospitality in a modern, relaxed setting. Golf, swimming, tennis, sand volleyball, fishing and boating can all be enjoyed during a stay at this Scottsdale hotel. The property features breathtaking views of McCormick Ranch Golf Club, Camelback Lake, the McDowell Mountains, and Camelback Mountain.
