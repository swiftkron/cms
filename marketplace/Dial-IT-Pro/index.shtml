Dial IT Pro

Connect Act! with your telephony system and save time, optimize processes and collect the necessary customer-related information effectively. The telephone, besides email, is the communication medium most widely used for daily tasks.

DIAL|IT integrates the telephone almost completely into Act! and saves considerable working time compared to the manual transfer of a telephone number from the screen to the phone keypad. Any formatting errors in the telephone numbers are automatically detected and corrected by DIAL|IT, and input errors are eliminated.

You can call your Act! Contacts using Skype and the available phone system directly from your Act! database with help of integrated phone system.

The following features are available with DIAL|IT Professional:

  • Easy customization of DIAL|IT-toolbar in detailed view and list view of Contacts
  • Call a contact from the Company-detail view or company-list view
  • Speed-/direct dial
  • Call Secondary Contacts
  • Calling from clipboard
  • Calling via Skype
  • Calling via available phone system
  • Sending text message using Skype
  • Personal caller identification
  • Pop-up window with Contact information for incoming call
  • Record the notes and plan the future activities while talking to your business partner
  • Automatic display of Contact details for incoming call
  • Automatic display of the last Contact after the call
  • Record a History during the call
  • Creating an Activity during the call
  • Displays scheduled calls as to-do list, call directly from here
  • Automatic planning of Activities / creation of History for missed calls
  • Spelling check in text fields
  • Creating a call list
  • Adding previously unknown numbers to existing Contacts
  • Send e-mail- and report templates during a telephone conversation
  • Add/remove a Contact from a Group during the conversation
  • Create e-mail templates for different clients
  • Create Contacts from previously unknown numbers
  • Multi Company dialing (multi-client capability)
  • Central DIAL|IT configuration
