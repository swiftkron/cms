Outreach|IT

Sending personalized email newsletters is one of the most effective form of direct marketing. By doing so, you remain in the memory of your customers and keep your database up to date at the same time – quickly, easily and inexpensively. However, the default Act! email connector quickly reaches its limits when required to send e-mails to large groups of customers and handle recipient rejected emails.

A special advantage of OUTREACH|IT is the advanced planning ability. This allows you to decide and determine in detail when, by whom, to whom and with which content your emails should be sent. This allows a more targeted customer approach and offers better timing for potential post-editing processes. OUTREACH|IT features a newsletter-workflow. You can define time intervals at which your emails should be sent to the Contacts in a Group, based on different templates. The Contacts can then be moved automatically to other Groups.

The following features are coming with OUTREACH|IT:

  • Sending already created e-newsletters, editorial ads with a lot of text, event invitations, letters, product information, announcements and much more.
  • Sending messages using multiple email accounts which are assigned to the respective dataset managers.
  • Sending time-controlled emails automatically to the Contacts of a Group directly after the Contact has been assigned.
  • Sending emails in small packages to avoid the overload or restriction of the server or the email provider.
  • Improved History display
  • Ability to choose recipients based on various filter criteria.
  • Activities start automatically after emailing
  • Sending multiple emails in defined time intervals.
  • Adding emails rejected by recipients automatically to a special Group for proper processing
