AddressGrabber Business

AddressGrabber Business is a single-click data entry and activity scheduler that automatically extracts and enters contact details into Act!. It also transfers addresses between Act! and shipping/accounting applications and schedules activities such as calls, meetings, and to-do's for incoming leads.

  • Enters addresses from various sources into Act! with one click – e-mail signatures, Web pages, online yellow and white pages, web search results, CD directories, text files, and more
  • Extracts contact fields like Name, Salutation, Title, Company, Address, City, State, Zip, Country, Phone, Ext, Mobile Phone, E-mail, and URL
  • Verifies addresses with online US Postal Services database before transferring it into Act!
  • Creates Contacts and links contacts to existing Company record
  • Creates a Company record if the company record does not exist
  • Finds and updates contact information in an existing Act! record
  • Transfers addresses to Business and/or Home Address section of Act!
  • Quickly transfers contact details to Accounting (QuickBooks), Shipping (UPS, FedEx) and Online Shipping applications

AddressGrabber Business System Requirements

  • Sage ACT! Pro 2013/Act! Pro v16 or Sage ACT! Premium 2013/Act! Premium v16
  • Pentium or equivalent recommended
  • Microsoft® Windows® XP/2000, Windows Vista™
  • 128 MB RAM or as required by OS
  • 105 MB of available hard disk space to install the program
