FETCH|IT Basic

FETCH|IT offers the ability for your Company to take advantage of webforms which are sent by email. It will automatically import all needed data to your CRM system and will save time spent manually processing webforms. FETCH|IT will eliminate human errors which may occur while transferring the data. The data records will be checked for duplicates and updated if necessary. FETCH|IT also lets you specify minimal data import requirements to protect your CRM system from rubbish data.

The most important features are as follows:

  • Import online web forms to your CRM system
  • Activities planning with Contacts
  • Saving web forms information
  • Contact categorization
  • Duplicate checking
  • Optional: saving or removing processed online web forms
  • Importing data to Contacts, Companies and Groups
  • Adding Documents to Companies and Contacts

FETCH|IT works in combination with IMPORT|IT and SERVICE|IT add-ons which are included in the license price.

System Requirements

System requirements are the same as for your Act! system. Supports Act! version starting with Act! 2009.
