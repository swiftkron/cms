Handheld Contact

Handheld Contact™ for Act! makes it simple to go mobile with the power to wirelessly access and manage your Act! Contacts, Calendar, Activites, and more right on your mobile device - even if you have no Internet connection.

Easy to setup, easy to use, and easy to manage users - Handheld Contact™ gives you access to the Act! features that matter.

  • Freedom of wireless syncing - if there is no Internet connection, your device will update the next time it connects.
  • Track every interaction - whenever you call, email, or text message a contact, Handheld Contact™ automatically creates an Act! history record for you and asks for details.
  • Never miss a meeting - your smartphone or tablet will alert you of all your scheduled Act! Alarms.
  • Top-notch collaboration on the go - Handheld Contact™ automatically keeps all Act! computers or laptops and all Handheld Contact™ powered mobile devices up-to-date with the latest details.
