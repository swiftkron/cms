Bulk Change IT

BULKCHANGE|IT gives you the ability to update your Act! Opportunities fields, copy them and assign to Contacts fast and easily. It saves your time and facilitates your Company workflow. The following features are available with BULKCHANGE|IT add-on:

  • Function Extenstion. Performs bulk changes in sales Oppotunity fields (Act! permits changes of the Contact field only).
  • Facilitates your workflow. Adjusts data in one step.
  • Time Saving. Updates several data fields in data-sets simultaneously.
  • Targeted Editing. Applies changes to the current sales Opportunity, current look up, or all sales Opportunities.
  • Transparency. Sets the order of implementation for the selected changes.
  • Diverse Requirements. Modifies and copies sales Opportunities by defining a condition, e.g. “if-then” logic.

System Requirements

  • Sage ACT! Pro 2012/Sage ACT! Pro 2013 or Sage ACT! Premium 2012/Sage ACT! Premium 2013
  • Language: English, German
  • Memory: 2.0 GB of RAM (recommended)
