Handheld Contact (Pocket PC or Treo Smartphone) – 1 year service

Handheld Contact puts your favorite Act! features on the Pocket PC or Treo Smartphone, and our wireless synchronization service keeps your Pocket PC or Treo Smartphone and Act! database always up-to-date throughout the day!

One month of service

  • Have full usability in and out of wireless coverage. Act! data is stored on the Pocket PC or Treo Smartphone making it fully usable outside of wireless coverage areas.
  • Collaborate with team members using 2-way wireless sync. Team members using Act! on their PC are synchronized wirelessly to the Pocket PC or Treo Smartphone in minutes – remote users can receive planned or last minute meetings, calls, to-do items, and relevant contact details.
  • Synchronize 26 pre-defined contact fields and 50 user-defined fields, with up to 30 Act! notes and history items, allowing users to select which types of notes to synchronize.
