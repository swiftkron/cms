Act! Cloud Essentials Library

View a selection of online training videos to learn the essential tasks within Act!. Whether you want to explore Act! features or train new employees, you can do this with a click of a button.

  • Manage relationships by keeping everything in one organized place from phone numbers, emails, conversation notes, meeting and activity history, documents, social media profiles, and more!
  • Use a variety of search tools to instantly access the exact customer relationship details you need.
  • Increase productivity by scheduling new activities, automating routine tasks with Smart Tasks, and tracking past activities as history within each customer’s relationship details.
  • Integrate with Microsoft Outlook to create contacts, create activities, and attach messages to contacts all from your Inbox!
  • Sell more by managing leads from first interaction through close using out-of-the-box sales stages or a process you customize.
  • Measure results using 50+ reports and dashboards, or for deeper analysis, export data to Microsoft® Excel®.
  • Share relationship details across your business with data-sharing and Synchronization options for teams of multiple sizes.
