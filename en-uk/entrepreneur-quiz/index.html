You’re the kind of entrepreneur whose vision changes the entire world. You pride yourself on integrity, drive and philanthropy. You want your business to be immensely successful but you also want to help people.

Learn from the likes of Bill Gates and Elon Musk. You need to be intelligent, determined and a good employer with a clear vision for the future. If you ensure your business is not solely for personal gain, you’ll watch it flourish.

You’re a web superstar! The internet is booming and you’ve got a keen eye for the next big thing. You’re clued up on digital trends and ready to launch the next big site or app.

Just like Mark Zuckerberg, you’re a savvy tech expert who is abreast of the latest changes in the world of technology. You have a keen eye for innovation and are always looking for new ways to harness your talent.

You’re never one to rest on your laurels. Each project is something to be championed, made into the best it can be and expanded. You move from idea to idea, turning everything to gold as you go. Just like Sir Richard Branson, you’re a natural born leader.

To succeed, you’ll be a master at multitasking. You’ll bring new ideas, techniques and strategies to the forefront of your business and brand. Like Mr Branson, you should be quick to spot gaps in the market and think outside of the box.

You’re a true sales and business expert who always looks to drive forward success. You want to own a chain of profitable ventures and will stop at nothing to get them.

If you’re looking to launch your own retail empire, you’ll need a head for sales. Stick to simple principles like Theo Paphitis, who argues that the K.I.S.S (Keep It Simple Stupid) method is the best. Your education is secondary to your hard work – you’ll work your way up through a company to gain experience before launching your own ventures.

You’re a creative-minded person who thrives on imagination. You love the idea of being your own boss, policing your own time and turning your dreams into reality. Your vision will bring something fresh and unique into the business world.

If you’re a creative, there’s no substitute for hard work. Take inspiration from successful entrepreneurs like Arianna Huffington and work tirelessly to create a tangible business model.

You’re a workaholic but on your own terms. From the desk in your bedroom, you’re the kind of person who can make millions from the tiniest spark of imagination. You set your own schedule, have no employees to worry about and focus on creating the next viral hit.

To follow in the footsteps of Evan Spiegel, you’ll need a solid set of coding and design skills and a keen eye for trends. You can test a few different products until you hit the right one, before riding the wave of success from your bedroom to a multi-million pound home.
